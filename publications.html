[C.3, ID] Structure-aware Adaptive Kernel MPPCA Denoising for Diffusion MRI
Ananya Singhal, Dattesh Dayanand Shanbhag, Sudhanya Chatterjee
Gradient-informed structural prior that dynamically adjusts kernel geometry per-voxel for high b-value diffusion MRI. 18% SNR gain with 96% tissue boundary preservation vs. fixed-size MPPCA baselines on HCP data at b-values of 5000–10000 s/mm².

[C.2] ARMARecon: ARMA Convolutional Filter Based GNN for Neurodegenerative Dementia Classification
Venkata Sesha, Ananya Singhal, Saurabh J. Shigwan, Nitin Kumar
Novel GNN integrating ARMA spectral filtering with reconstruction-driven regularisation — first approach to jointly address over-smoothing and over-squashing in sparse-label neuroimaging graphs. 98.3% CN vs. MCI, 99.7% CN vs. AD on ADNI dataset.

Abhishek Tiwari, Ananya Singhal, Saurabh J. Shigwan, Rajeev Kumar Singh

[C.1] Early Diagnosis of Alzheimer through Swin-Transformer-Based Deep Learning Framework using Sparse Diffusion Measures
Abhishek Tiwari, Ananya Singhal, Saurabh J. Shigwan, Rajeev Kumar Singh
Swin-Transformer framework generating FA, AxD, and MD measurements using just 5 and 21 diffusion directions. Outperforms traditional linear least square methods while reducing scanning time by more than half on ADNI dataset.
