About Me

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student at Shiv Nadar Institute of Eminence with a minor in Mathematics, graduating in May 2024. My academic journey has been marked by consistent excellence, earning me a place on the Dean's List with a GPA of 8.41/10.

My research focuses on applying deep learning techniques to medical imaging, particularly in the early diagnosis of neurological conditions using diffusion MRI. I've published work at prestigious conferences including ICCV and ACML, and have professional experience at GE Healthcare, Dell Technologies, and ZKTeco.

I'm passionate about building innovative solutions, as evidenced by my projects like MentorME and Diama. Beyond technical skills, I've been recognized with awards including the Generation Google Scholarship (APAC) and the Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Travel Grant.
